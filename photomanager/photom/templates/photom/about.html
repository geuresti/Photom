About SPI Photom

This website was developed by Giovanni Euresti. You can contact me at gioeuresti123@gmail.com for business inquiries.

If you're having issues with the website please reach out to Jennifer at jen@signaturephotographyinc.com.

The goal of the SPI Photo Manager tool is to simplify the transfer of student photos from photographer through the school to the parents. This website is an easy-to-use hub for schools and SPI to manage the digital distribution of student potratraits.

Photom was made using Django for the front/backend and Postgresql for the database. The languages used are Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Django's template language.
