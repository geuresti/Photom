About Photom

This website was developed by Giovanni Euresti. You can contact me at gioeuresti123@gmail.com for business inquiries.

Photom was a commissioned project I made in order to simplify to transfer of photos from photographer through the school to the parents. This project allows for the easy digital distribution of student photos which is something that school photographers do not have widely available or easily afforadble.

With Photom it's simple; the school account will populate their dashboard with classes and students and the photographer will populate the student pages with the relevant photos. The school account can then go in and download and distribute the digital photos to parents as requested.
